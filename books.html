The Case for Christ Graduate Edition: A Journalist’s Personal Investigation of the Evidence for Jesus

The Case for Christ Graduate Edition: A Journalist’s Personal Investigation of the Evidence for Jesus by Lee Strobel is a powerful invitation to embark on a life-changing journey of faith. As a skeptical journalist, Strobel set out to disprove Christianity — but what he discovered transformed his heart and mind forever. In this special edition for graduates, he shares his personal story of relentless pursuit and unexpected discovery, offering clear, compelling evidence for the truth of Jesus Christ. Thoughtfully written for those stepping into a new chapter of life, this book encourages readers to seek answers with courage, think deeply, and embrace the life of purpose and hope found in Christ. It’s not just a case to be solved — it’s a relationship to be discovered.

The book is a compelling exploration of the historical, scientific, and philosophical evidence surrounding the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Written specifically for students stepping into adulthood, this edition adapts Strobel’s classic bestseller to address the questions and doubts that often arise during the transition from school to the broader world. As an award-winning journalist and former skeptic, Strobel retraces his own journey from disbelief to faith, interviewing top scholars and experts along the way. Clear, thoughtful, and engaging, this book invites readers to examine the facts and challenges them to make an informed decision about who Jesus truly is.

Tortured for Christ

Tortured for Christ by Richard Wurmbrand is a powerful testimony of a pastor’s unwavering faith amid extreme persecution. Imprisoned and tortured for his belief in Christ, Wurmbrand’s story is one of incredible suffering, yet remarkable strength and hope. His experience shows that even in the darkest times, God’s love and presence can sustain the soul. This book is an inspiring reminder of the true cost of following Christ and the unbreakable power of faith.

Imprisoned and brutally tortured for preaching the Gospel in communist Romania, Wurmbrand’s courage shines through as a beacon of hope and love that no force on earth could break. His testimony is a powerful reminder that no matter the trials we face, God’s presence can transform pain into purpose. Tortured for Christ is not just a story of survival; it is a call to embrace the strength and courage that come from a deep, unshakable faith in Christ. It’s a book that inspires believers to stand firm, even in the face of adversity, knowing that God’s love will always prevail.
