About

Joena San Diego is the CEO and Founder of JSD Books & Publishing, a thriving publishing company based in the Philippines. With a heart for storytelling and a passion for purpose-driven literature, Joena has become a prominent figure in the Filipino literary and entrepreneurial scene.

She holds a Master’s degree from the prestigious University of the Philippines – Diliman Campus, where she honed her academic expertise and deepened her commitment to meaningful communication. Before founding her own company, Joena gained extensive experience in book authoring and the publishing industry, equipping her with the knowledge and insight to lead her business with excellence.

At Books & Publishing, Joena oversees all facets of the company, from product development to marketing strategy. Under her leadership, the company has grown into a platform that empowers both new and established writers to share their stories with the world.

Her works are celebrated for their depth, vulnerability, and authenticity, often touching on themes of love, healing, faith, and self-discovery.

Joena is also the founder of Pieces and Jewelry , a sister company that reflects her creativity and entrepreneurial spirit in the world of fashion and self-expression. Through both her businesses, Joena continues to inspire others—especially women—to live intentionally, embrace their stories, and build with purpose.

With an ever-growing influence in literature and business, Joena San Diego is not just a CEO or an author—she is a beacon of hope, resilience, and empowerment for the Filipino community.

As a prolific author herself, Joena has written numerous inspirational and soul-stirring books, including:
