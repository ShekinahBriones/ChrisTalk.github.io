About

Mariann Lopera is a passionate Filipino writer, blogger, and speaker devoted to sharing the love of Jesus through words that inspire and uplift. In 2017, she launched her blog, She Writes For Jesus, as a space to pour out encouraging reflections, faith-filled messages, and Biblical truths that draw people closer to God and His Word.

With a heart for young adults, Mariann authored the book Work In Progress: Navigating Life As An Adult—a thoughtful guide meant to inspire and equip the next generation to glorify God as they journey through the often overwhelming seasons of adulthood.

Her calling has led her to speak at youth events across various churches, where she boldly points others to the Gospel. Despite living with a disability, Mariann’s life is a testimony of how God’s power is made perfect in weakness. Her story reminds others that God can use anyone—regardless of limitations—for His glory.

Mariann writes not for fame, but for faith. Her life and words echo her simple yet powerful belief. Through her writing and speaking, Mariann continues to inspire young adults to pursue Jesus, embrace their calling, and trust that God can use anyone for His glory—even in seasons of waiting. She believes that her life is not about what she can do for God, but about what God can do through her.
