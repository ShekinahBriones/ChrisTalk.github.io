Jesus Image

About

In a world longing for hope, truth, and love, Jesus Image stands as a beacon of light, devoted to bringing the presence of Jesus to the nations. Rooted in a deep passion for Christ, this ministry seeks to transform lives through the power of the Holy Spirit. More than just an organization, Jesus Image is a movement—one that desires to see hearts set on fire for Jesus and His love permeate every aspect of life.

Join us in worship, teaching, and spreading His love everywhere.

At the core of Jesus Image is a simple yet profound mission: to bring Jesus to the world. This mission is not accomplished through mere words but through a deep, tangible experience of His presence. Jesus Image Church, Jesus School, and Jesus Image events serve as avenues where people encounter the living God. Whether through intimate worship, powerful teachings, or Spirit-filled gatherings, the ministry’s heart remains the same—to glorify Jesus and lead others into a life-changing relationship with Him.

One of the most significant aspects of Jesus Image is its global reach. Millions of people from various nations have joined in live-streamed meetings and worship gatherings. In an age where technology connects the world, Jesus Image uses this platform to spread the Gospel beyond physical boundaries. Through digital ministry, countless lives have been touched, healed, and transformed as they experience the power of Jesus from their homes.

However, the ministry does not stop at online engagement. Jesus School equips believers to walk in the fullness of God’s calling, raising up a generation that burns with love for Jesus and carries His presence wherever they go. The teachings emphasize intimacy with God, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the call to evangelize. Through discipleship and training, Jesus Image ensures that the message of Christ is not only received but carried forward by those who are deeply transformed.

Ultimately, Jesus Image is not about programs, events, or platforms—it is about a Person: Jesus Christ. Everything the ministry does is centered on loving Him, worshiping Him, and making Him known. As lives are touched and hearts are set ablaze, the ripple effect continues, drawing more people into the love of Christ. In a world desperately seeking answers, Jesus Image offers the only answer that truly satisfies—the presence of Jesus.

Through the power of the Holy Spirit, Jesus Image continues to fulfill its mission of bringing Jesus to the world. It is a reminder that revival is not just an event but a lifestyle—one where Jesus is at the center, and His love transforms every heart it touches.
