About

The Proclamation Coalition is a Christian organization committed to spreading the Gospel through creative resources, apparel, and tools that inspire conversations about faith and unity. Their mission focuses on equipping individuals with the means to share the message of Christ in a way that resonates with modern culture. With a diverse selection of Christian clothing and resources, they aim to foster unity among believers and spark meaningful dialogue centered around faith, love, and salvation.

Through their products, Proclamation Coalition encourages Christians to wear their faith proudly and engage others in discussions about the hope found in Jesus Christ. By providing high-quality, purpose-driven merchandise, the coalition aims to empower believers to use their creativity as a tool for evangelism, bringing people together to make a lasting impact on the world. Whether it's through a shirt that shares the Gospel message or resources that equip individuals for spiritual growth, Proclamation Coalition seeks to strengthen the body of Christ and build a more unified Christian community.

Their ultimate goal is to create a culture where faith is openly shared, and the love of Christ transcends boundaries. The Proclamation Coalition is dedicated to fostering spiritual growth and connection, ensuring that the Gospel reaches every corner of the world.
